About AshxMoto

Blender Modeling, Real-Time Rendering & Web Integration

Motivation & Concept

AshxMoto was created as a passion project combining my love for motorcycles, design, and interactive web experiences. The idea was to present customized bikes and gear in an immersive 3D environment — blending aesthetic form with digital function.

Tools & Workflow

All models were crafted in Blender, with animations and shaders fine-tuned manually. I exported them as .glb files to preserve performance and animations. For the front-end rendering, I used Three.js, applying lighting, orbit controls, and animation handlers to bring each bike and helmet to life.

The project also includes sound integration, lighting tweaks via GUI, animation triggering, and full camera control — all designed to replicate a dynamic showroom feel.

Credits & Acknowledgments

All models are designed by me from scratch using Blender. The rendering is powered by Three.js. Special thanks to online communities that helped me debug and push this project further. Sound was recorded using my friend's old bike and edited to loop.
